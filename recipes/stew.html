Egg Stew Recipe

Description

Today, I am going to show you how to make egg stew for one person to last roughly a week. Find below the ingredients you'll need.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Kpakpo Shito
  • Dawadawa
  • Red pepper
  • Maggi
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Blend two onions and four tomatoes
  2. Add salt, kpakpo shito, dawadawa, red pepper, ginger and garlic to the mix
  3. Pour oil in a suacepan and cook tomato paste for 5 minutes
  4. Add your blended sauce to the cooked paste
  5. Add cut onions and other vegetables of your choice and let it cook
  6. Add Maggi and stir continually
  7. Add however number of eggs you want
  8. Leave for about 10 minutes and stir continually

There you go, you've made egg stew!

It pairs very nicely with jollof rice and chicken
