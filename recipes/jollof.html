Jollof Recipe

Description

Today, I am going to show you how to make (Ghanaian) Jollof rice for one person to last roughly a week. Find below the ingredients you'll need.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Kpakpo Shito
  • Dawadawa
  • Red pepper
  • Maggi
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Blend two onions and four tomatoes
  2. Add salt, kpakpo shito, dawadawa, red pepper, ginger and garlic to the mix
  3. Pour oil in a suacepan and cook tomato paste for 5 minutes
  4. Add your blended sauce to the cooked paste
  5. Add cut onions and other vegetables of your choice and let it cook
  6. Add Maggi and stir continually
  7. Add rice until three quarters full
  8. Stir continually on medium heat until rice is soft and voila!

There you go, you've made Ghanaian jollof. For Nigerian jollof... you're going to have to ask them lol.

It pairs very nicely with chicken
