Oven-Baked Chicken Recipe

Description

Today, I am going to show you how to make oven-baked chicken! It is for one person to last roughly a week. Find below the ingredients you'll need.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Kpakpo Shito
  • Red pepper

Steps

  1. Wash chicken breasts thoroughly and pat dry with tissue paper
  2. Pour salt, kpakpo shito, red pepper, ginger and garlic on them
  3. Allow it to marinate for at least 6 hours
  4. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  5. Bake chicken for 45 minutes, turning halfway

There you go, you've made oven-baked chicken! It pairs very nicely with jollof rice
